Get ready for an unforgettable evening at our Christmas Dinner event! The night is filled with delightful activities, starting with a captivating cocktail hour from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM. At 7:30 PM, a sumptuous dinner awaits you, promising to tantalize your taste buds until 9:30 PM. But the surprises don't end there. Be sure to stay until 10:00 PM for a magical finale that will leave you in awe. Throughout the night, enjoy the melodious tunes of live music and join us for heartwarming carol singing. What's more, we have renowned speakers lined up to share their insights and add to the holiday spirit. It's an enchanting journey through the spirit of Christmas that you won't want to miss.
